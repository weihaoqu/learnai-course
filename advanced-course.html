Phase 13 — Required

Tools & Protocols: MCP (Model Context Protocol) / A2A (Agent-to-Agent)

~17hours
23 lessons

Learning Objectives

  • Design safe tool interfaces, protocol adapters, and agent-to-agent communication contracts.
  • Build MCP-style tool servers with schema discipline, permissions, and auditability.
  • Evaluate protocol choices for portability, security, and operational maintainability.

Ethics, Safety & Alignment

Capstone Projects
